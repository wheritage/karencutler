About Karen

Encouragement, not pressure.

I became a trainer because I believe fitness should feel good — physically and emotionally. My job isn't to push you until it hurts; it's to help you build strength, confidence, and habits that actually last.

Every body is different, so every plan is too. We'll work together on movement that feels right for you, at a pace that keeps you motivated and coming back with a smile.

Specialized

Training through menopause & beyond.

Your body changes through perimenopause and menopause — and your training should change with it. With the right approach, this stage can be one of the strongest of your life.

I help women train smart through hormonal shifts: protecting bone and joint health, keeping energy and sleep steady, and building real, lasting strength.
